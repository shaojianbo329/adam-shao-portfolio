# Visual Style Language

The uploaded references combine a poster-like editorial rhythm with disciplined technical structure. Their strongest signals are:

- Oversized typography used as the primary visual anchor.
- High-contrast composition with generous negative space.
- Clear modular grids, thin linework, and precise alignment.
- Minimal navigation details with small, confident labels.
- Restrained color atmosphere, using one accent only where it matters.
- Visual depth through layering, not heavy decoration.

For this personal portfolio, the style is reinterpreted rather than copied:

- The hero is name-first and portrait-led, so Adam's identity is the immediate brand signal.
- The palette moves from the reference's black/pink poster tone into navy, white, gray, and cool neutral surfaces for a more academic international profile.
- The section system uses large editorial headings, fine borders, and grid-based modules.
- Motion stays quiet: load-in reveals, scroll reveals, hover lift, button transitions, and subtle portrait depth.
- The website avoids invented claims. Resume-derived facts are separated from current research interests.
